Our Story

A Family of Faith & Craft

Rooted in Sarafa Bazar, Lashkar, Gwalior, Gargi Jewellers began as a small family workshop and has grown into a name trusted for two things: the purity of our gold and silver, and the care we put into every idol and ornament that leaves our hands.

We believe divinity has many faces. Alongside our gold and silver jewellery, we hand-finish frames of gods and goddesses from every tradition — because every home's altar deserves the same craftsmanship, whichever faith it honours.

Maa Kali Kripa — every piece leaves our workshop with a blessing.
